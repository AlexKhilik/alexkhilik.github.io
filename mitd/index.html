Monsters Hate Light

Expose monsters to the light to destroy them! Fun physics puzzle game.

Clear the darkness and eliminate the creepy monsters in Monsters Hate Light, a captivating physics-based puzzle game!

These pesky little creatures are lurking in the shadows, and they have one fatal weakness: light. Your mission is to clear them out by using your brain, interacting with the environment, and letting the laws of physics do the rest. Push them into the glowing light zones and watch them pop! The best part? You can either roll the monsters into the light, or roll the glowing light orbs directly to the monsters.

How to play:

Clear the path to victory by interacting with various objects. Break stone bricks, toss away wooden crates, slice ropes, and strategically detonate bombs to expose the monsters to the light. Every level is a unique logic puzzle waiting to be solved.

Key Features:

  • 💡 Unique Light Mechanics: Light is your ultimate weapon — and it's a physical object you can push and roll!
  • 🧠 Brain-Teasing Physics: Realistic gravity and object interactions.
  • 💣 Interactive Environment: Cut ropes, explode bombs, and smash blocks to create a path.
  • 🎯 Creative Solutions: Move the monster to the light, or bring the light to the monster!
  • 💥 Satisfying Action: Watch the monsters pop and disappear when the light hits them.
  • 🕹️ Play Anywhere: A perfect offline puzzle game to train your brain on the go.
